11 - Translate integers to English.

Problem
Given 1 integer per line, print the integer in English.

Input
There will be multiple lines with one integer between 0 and 9.

Output
For each line of input, print on separate lines the name of the integer in English with lower case letters.  

Sample Input
1
2
3
4
5
6
7
8
9
0

Sample Output
one
two
three
four
five
six
seven
eight
nine
zero
