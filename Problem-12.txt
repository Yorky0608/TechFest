12 - lowercase

Rewrite words so they have all lower-case letters, except words that begin with the letter Q.

Problem
Read in a word and convert it to all lower-case letters.  Do not convert words that begin with the letter Q.

Input
There will be multiple lines with a single word on each line.

Output
For each line of input, print on the transformed word on a separate line.  

Sample Input
First
subseQuent
neXT
QuiT

Sample Output
first
subsequent
next
QuiT
