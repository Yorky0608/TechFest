13 - Test passwords for strength.

Problem
Read in a password and judge if it is good enough.  A good password will have at least one lowercase letter, uppercase letter, and number.  A good password will also be at least 8 characters long.

Input
There will be multiple lines with a single password on each line.

Output
For each line of input, print "Good" or "Bad" on a separate line, depending on the strength of the password.

Sample Input
First1
subseQuent
next1
CorrectHorse1

Sample Output
Bad
Bad
Bad
Good
