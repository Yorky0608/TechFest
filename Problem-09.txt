09 - Length

Find the length of a phrase.

Problem
Read a string and print out how long it is.

Input
There will be multiple lines with one or more words, numbers and spaces.

Output
For each line of input, print on separate lines the length of words, numbers and spaces.

Sample Input
one two
three four
five six
seven 7 eight 8
nine zero

Sample Output
7
10
8
15
9
