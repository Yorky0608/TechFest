10 - No Space

Find the length of a phrase, less the spaces.

Problem
Read a string, ignore the spaces, and print out how long it is.

Input
There will be multiple lines with one or more words, numbers and spaces.

Output
For each line of input, print on separate lines the length of words and numbers but do not count the spaces.  

Sample Input
one two
three four
five six
seven 7 eight 8
nine zero

Sample Output
6
9
7
12
8
